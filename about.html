About

Welcome to Rebecca Alexander Hair Designs!
I'm Rebecca Alexander, your expert in transformative hairstyling, with a passion honed over nearly three decades. Beginning my journey with a dream at the Carsten Institute in Arizona, under Carsten Wilms, I've developed a unique approach that marries innovative techniques with classic elegance.

Every service I offer reflects my dedication to excellence and a 29-year commitment to making each client look their best. Whether you're after a bold change, a subtle update, or a chance to rediscover your style, I offer a spectrum of possibilities.

Let's celebrate your individuality together. I'll craft a look that captures attention and truly represents you, using your hair as my canvas to create pure artistry.

Book now for a memorable styling experience with Rebecca Alexander, where your hair narrates your story.
